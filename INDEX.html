Computer Science

Computer science is the study of algorithmic processes, computing machines, and computation itself. As a discipline, computer science courses encompass a wide range of subjects, from the theoretical study of algorithms, computation, and information to practical problems of implementing computing systems in hardware and software. Computer science being an expansive field in the IT sector has led to a growth of many courses in computer science at all levels of study such as undergraduate, postgraduate, certification, doctorate, etc. Some of the best computer science courses are BTech Computer Science, Diploma in Computer Science, MTech Computer Science Engineering, etc. Check: Best Certificate Courses in Computer Science Eligibility criteria to pursue diploma and undergraduate programs in computer science is to secure a 50% aggregate score in class 12 exams and opt for Science (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics) stream. The eligibility criteria to pursue a master's or PhD course in computer science is to have a bachelor’s or master’s degree in computer science or related fields with more than 50% aggregate score respectively.


Mobile Application Devlopment

Mobile or Smartphones have become the most useful devices in this digital era. Nowadays, it transforms the whole world into a small global village. The course Mobile App Development, is about the development process of smart phones or other handheld devices. Mobile App (Application) Development course deals with the processes of developing applications for mobiles, smart phones, tablets, etc. The course covers the topics like, software development, mobile application development, web app development, mobile computing, android multimedia, iOS multimedia, app safety and security, app functionality, etc. There is no such eligibility to learn this course. But for the diploma, degree programs one must have to pass the 12th in any discipline (arts, science) from a recognized board. The employees of a company or industry also can learn this course. Some of the top course providers of Mobile App Development courses are Udemy, edX, Coursera, etc. The online courses can be learned remotely from all over the world.
